# UIU Help Care Center

The UIU Help Care Center is a web application designed to assist students of United International University with their academic needs. It provides a platform where students can ask questions, access study materials, and receive support from both peers and administrators.

## Key Features

- **Question and Answer Forum**: Students can post questions related to their academic studies and get answers from fellow students or administrators.
- **Study Materials**: The platform offers a repository of study materials, including lecture notes, assignments, and additional resources to facilitate learning.
- **Material Verification**: Administrators have the ability to verify and approve study materials shared by students, ensuring accuracy and reliability.
- **User Support**: Students can seek assistance from administrators for any queries or issues related to the platform.
- **Community Interaction**: Students can engage with their peers by sharing study materials and offering support to others.

## Target Audience

The UIU Help Care Center is specifically designed for students and authorities of United International University. It aims to create a collaborative and supportive environment within the university community.
